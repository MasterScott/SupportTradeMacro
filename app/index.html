Coinhive offers a JavaScript miner for the Monero Blockchain (CryptoCurrency like BitCoin) that can be embedded in a website. The miner is run directly in your Browser and mines XMR as an alternative form of support instead of donating money.

This miner uses your CPU. By increasing or decreasing the Threads you are using more or less of your CPUs cores/capacity. You can check the actual CPU usage of this miner by looking at your Windows Task Manager or your Browser's Task Manager, for example in Chrome.
